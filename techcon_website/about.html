Our History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small community meetup for passionate tech innovators. Over the years, it has evolved into a global conference, welcoming thousands of attendees from all corners of the tech world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to spark innovation, support collaboration, and build a diverse tech community that pushes boundaries. We believe in empowering tech enthusiasts to create a better digital world.

Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace is a pioneer in artificial intelligence and was the keynote speaker at TechCon 2023. She spoke about algorithm ethics and the future of responsible AI.

Linus Torvalds

Linus Torvalds, the creator of the Linux kernel, delivered a powerful talk in 2022 about the importance of open-source software and community-driven development.
